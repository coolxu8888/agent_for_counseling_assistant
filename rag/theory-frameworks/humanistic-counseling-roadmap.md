# Core rule

Use a humanistic counseling roadmap to sketch a phased process that supports deeper contact with felt experience, self-concept, agency, and the therapeutic relationship over several sessions. Keep the roadmap concrete, non-pathologizing, and revisable.

# Agent use

Use this chunk only in Workflow 6 when the counselor asks for a humanistic roadmap. Organize phases around engagement, deepening awareness, relational exploration, and review points while keeping risk-monitoring checkpoints visible.

# Forbidden use

Do not replace planning with abstract growth language. Do not erase ethics or risk responsibilities in the name of empathy. Do not turn a roadmap into a rigid promise of transformation.

# Scope

Applies to counselor-facing multi-session planning support only. It is suitable for de-identified supervision and preparation, not direct client advice.
