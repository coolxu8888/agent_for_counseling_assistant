# Core rule

Use humanistic next-session planning to support one upcoming session that deepens felt experience, self-awareness, and relational safety without losing practical counselor boundaries. Keep the language concrete and grounded in the client's actual words.

# Agent use

Use this chunk only in Workflow 5 when the counselor asks for a humanistic next-session plan. Prioritize one session aim, empathic focus areas, process-oriented questions, and optional between-session reflection ideas that remain open to counselor judgment.

# Forbidden use

Do not replace planning with vague encouragement. Do not ignore risk, documentation, or professional boundaries in the name of warmth. Do not turn the next session into a multi-session growth roadmap.

# Scope

Applies to counselor-facing planning support for one upcoming session only. It is suitable for de-identified supervision and preparation, not direct client advice.
