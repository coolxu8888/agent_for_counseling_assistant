# Core rule

Use an integrative counseling roadmap to sequence assessment, hypothesis testing, support-building, and review over several sessions without pretending that one model fully explains the case. Keep the roadmap explicit about why each phase is being chosen.

# Agent use

Use this chunk only in Workflow 6 when the counselor asks for an integrative roadmap. Organize phases around priorities, missing information, protective factors, collaboration reminders, and risk-monitoring checkpoints while keeping the roadmap bounded and revisable.

# Forbidden use

Do not turn integrative language into a catch-all treatment package. Do not merge incompatible theories into certainty, and do not promise outcomes or fixed timelines.

# Scope

Applies to counselor-facing multi-session planning support only. It is suitable for de-identified supervision and preparation, not direct client advice.
