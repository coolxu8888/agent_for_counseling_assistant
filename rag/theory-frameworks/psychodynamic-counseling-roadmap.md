# Core rule

Use a psychodynamic counseling roadmap to propose a phased, tentative sequence for exploring patterns, affect regulation, defenses, and relational themes over several sessions. Keep the roadmap hypothesis-driven and revisable.

# Agent use

Use this chunk only in Workflow 6 when the counselor asks for a psychodynamic roadmap. Organize phases around engagement, pattern clarification, reflective exploration, and review checkpoints while preserving explicit risk and boundary monitoring.

# Forbidden use

Do not treat childhood or unconscious explanations as fact. Do not imply a fixed long-term treatment contract, guaranteed insight, or diagnosis. Do not overstate transference interpretations when the material is thin.

# Scope

Applies to counselor-facing multi-session planning support only. It is suitable for de-identified supervision and preparation, not direct client advice.
