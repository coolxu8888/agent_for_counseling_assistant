# Core rule

Use integrative next-session planning to organize one upcoming session around the most useful near-term focus while naming why that focus is being chosen from several possible lenses. Keep the plan bounded, transparent, and revisable.

# Agent use

Use this chunk only in Workflow 5 when the counselor asks for an integrative next-session plan. Prioritize one next-session goal, a small number of focus areas, collaboration reminders, and optional between-session tasks that remain subject to counselor judgment and case context.

# Forbidden use

Do not blend frameworks into vague generalities. Do not turn an integrative next-session plan into a phased roadmap, full treatment package, or diagnosis. Do not frame optional tasks as instructions the client must complete.

# Scope

Applies to counselor-facing planning support for one upcoming session only. It is suitable for de-identified supervision and preparation, not direct client advice.
