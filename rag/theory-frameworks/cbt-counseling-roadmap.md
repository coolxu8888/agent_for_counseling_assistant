# Core rule

Use a CBT counseling roadmap to sketch a revisable sequence of assessment, formulation refinement, skills practice, and review checkpoints across several sessions. Keep the roadmap flexible and tied to hypotheses that still need verification.

# Agent use

Use this chunk only in Workflow 6 when the counselor asks for a CBT roadmap. Organize phases around the cognitive-behavioral cycle, risk monitoring, and collaborative review points while keeping the immediate next session inside a broader multi-session structure.

# Forbidden use

Do not promise symptom change timelines, fixed session counts, or mandatory homework. Do not convert the roadmap into a diagnosis or pretend that exposure or restructuring is appropriate before readiness and safety are clarified.

# Scope

Applies to counselor-facing multi-session planning support only. It is suitable for de-identified supervision and preparation, not direct client advice.
